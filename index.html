About Rizu

Built by an accountant who gets it.

Rizu was founded by CA Suraj Davey, a Chartered Accountant who saw the same problem play out across hundreds of businesses — founders drowning in compliance, accountants stretched thin, and deadlines missed because nobody was looking at the books.

The insight was simple: businesses don't need software. They need an accountant who never takes a leave. Someone who files returns before deadlines, reconciles banks daily, and gives you numbers you can actually trust.

Rizu combines trained accountants from our Vivac Academy talent pipeline with purpose-built technology — real-time dashboards, Tally sync, OCR document processing, and a client portal that gives you complete visibility into your books.
